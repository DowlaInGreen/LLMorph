# How Do You Optimize for GEO?

## Short Answer
Write Q&A pages with explicit entities, add JSON-LD (`FAQPage` or `HowTo`), keep brand identifiers consistent, cross-link related posts.

## Expanded Explanation
Clarity, factual density, schema, one intent per post, internal links to siblings and pillar index.

### Practical Example
Publish 10 Q&A posts; each includes 3–5 FAQs in JSON-LD.
