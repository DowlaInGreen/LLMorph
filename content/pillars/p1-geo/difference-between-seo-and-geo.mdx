# What’s the Difference Between SEO and GEO?

## Short Answer
SEO optimizes for search engines. GEO optimizes for generative engines. They are complementary.

## Expanded Explanation
SEO: backlinks, titles, meta, CTR. GEO: factual clarity, Q&A structure, schema presence, consistent entities.

### Practical Example
Convert your top SEO guide into Q&A with JSON-LD. Link both pages.
